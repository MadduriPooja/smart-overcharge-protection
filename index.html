About the Project

The Problem: Overcharging

Overcharging occurs when a device remains plugged in after reaching 100%. This generates excess heat, degrades lithium-ion battery chemistry, and shortens the overall lifespan of your expensive gadgets.

The Solution: Smart Protection

The Smart Overcharge Protection System monitors battery health and charging cycles in real-time. It automatically disconnects the power flow when the optimal charge levels are reached, ensuring your battery stays "Green" for years to come.
